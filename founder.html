Sunilkumar

Founder of SRMIST Website

About the Founder

Welcome! I am Sunilkumar, the founder of the SRMIST website, a platform dedicated to empowering college students with free courses and certifications. My journey as a student and developer has driven me to create opportunities that bridge the gap between learning and application, ensuring that every student can access quality resources to grow personally and professionally.

Vision

My vision is to create an inclusive digital learning space where knowledge knows no boundaries. By providing free, high-quality courses and certifications, this platform aims to inspire students to explore new areas of learning and enhance their skillsets, preparing them for future challenges and opportunities.

Why I Started This Platform

As a college student, I understand the struggles of finding affordable yet valuable educational resources. This inspired me to build a platform for all students, regardless of their institution, to access skill-building courses and certification programs for free. I aim to make education accessible, impactful, and rewarding for everyone.

Core Values

  • Accessibility: Providing free learning opportunities to all.
  • Quality Education: Offering courses designed for real-world applications.
  • Recognition: Empowering students with certifications that add value to their careers.

A Note to Students

The SRMIST website is more than just a learning platform—it's a community of learners and achievers. Whether you're looking to explore a new field or build on existing knowledge, this platform is here to guide and support you every step of the way.

Let’s embark on this journey of growth together. Thank you for being part of our vision!
